# Lesson 6: Bulk Scheduler - Monthly Content Planning

**Duration**: 30 minutes  
**Goal**: Create and schedule an entire month's worth of content in 2 hours

---

## Why Bulk Scheduling?

**The Problem**:
- Daily content creation is time-consuming
- Easy to miss days
- Inconsistent posting hurts growth
- Stressful to create daily

**The Solution**:
- Create month of content in one session
- Automatic publishing
- Consistent posting
- Stress-free content creation

**Time Savings**: 
- Daily: 5 min × 30 days = 150 minutes
- Bulk: 2 hours = 120 minutes
- **Plus**: Better planning, less stress

---

## Two Ways to Access

### Method 1: Direct from Dashboard

1. Click "Bulk Scheduler" button
2. Access setup immediately
3. Start planning

**Best For**: Starting fresh monthly planning

---

### Method 2: From Faceless Video Generator

1. Create video normally
2. Reach Step 2 (Schedule Setup)
3. Toggle "Bulk Schedule" option
4. Guided through workflow

**Best For**: Converting single video to bulk

## Complete Workflow

### Step 1: Setup Options

**Number of Videos**:
- Choose how many to create
- Typically 30-60 for a month
- Based on posting frequency

**Start Date**:
- When to begin posting
- Can start immediately
- Or schedule for future

**Schedule Duration**:
- Up to 30 days maximum
- Set end date
- Plan entire month

**Posting Frequency**:
- **1 post per day**: 30 videos
- **2 posts per day**: 60 videos (recommended)
- **3+ posts per day**: 90+ videos (aggressive)
- **Custom schedule**: Your preference

**Pro Tip**: 2 posts per day (9 AM and 7 PM) is optimal for growth.

---

### Step 2: Video Configuration

**Video Style**:
- Choose preferred style
- Applied to all videos
- Consistent branding

**Voice & Language**:
- Select voice actor
- Choose language
- Consistent across all

**Other Settings**:
- Subtitle style
- Video length
- Aspect ratio
- All standard options

**Pro Tip**: Save your preferred settings to reuse.

---

### Step 3: Script Generation

**Enter Topic**:
- Main topic or niche
- Can be broad (e.g., "AI for business")
- AI creates variations

**AI Script Writer**:
- Automatically creates unique scripts
- Each video gets different script
- All based on your original topic
- Varied but related content

**How It Works**:
1. Enter main topic
2. AI generates 30-60 unique scripts
3. Each script is different
4. All related to your topic
5. Ready to create videos

**Pro Tip**: Use broad topics to get variety, or specific topics for focused content.

---

### Step 4: Generate Videos

**Review Summary**:
- Total number of videos
- Credits required
- Schedule overview
- Publishing timeline

**Generate Process**:
1. Click "Generate Videos"
2. System creates all videos
3. Redirected to Bulk Scheduler Tracker
4. Monitor progress

**Timeline**:
- 30 videos: ~1 hour
- 60 videos: ~2 hours
- Depends on video length

---

### Step 5: Monitor Progress

**Bulk Scheduler Tracker**:
- Dashboard to monitor all scheduled content
- Track each video's status
- See what's pending, processing, or published
- Adjust schedules if needed

**Features**:
- Real-time status updates
- Publishing timeline
- Video previews
- Edit capabilities

**Pro Tip**: Check tracker daily to ensure smooth publishing.

---

### Step 6: Automatic Publishing

**Once Generated**:
- Syllaby publishes automatically
- Based on channels, timing, frequency
- No manual intervention needed
- Content goes live on schedule

**What Happens**:
- Videos publish at scheduled times
- All platforms receive content
- Descriptions and tags auto-added
- CTAs included automatically

**Result**: Set and forget content publishing.

---

## Monthly Planning Session

### Sunday: Planning (30 minutes)

**Weekly Planning**:
1. Review last month's performance (10 min)
2. Identify top-performing content (5 min)
3. Plan topics for new month (10 min)
4. Prepare for bulk creation (5 min)

**Result**: Month planned, ready to create

---

### Monday: Creation (2 hours)

**Bulk Creation Session**:
1. Open Bulk Scheduler (5 min)
2. Configure all settings (10 min)
3. Generate scripts (5 min)
4. Create all videos (90 min)
5. Review and approve (10 min)

**Result**: Month of content created and scheduled

---

### Rest of Month: Maintenance

**Daily Tasks**:
- Monitor publishing (2 min)
- Engage with comments (5 min)
- Track performance (3 min)
- Create additional content if needed

**Result**: Consistent posting, minimal daily work

## Best Practices

### Planning

1. **Plan at Month Start**: Set aside 2 hours monthly
2. **Choose Optimal Times**: 9 AM and 7 PM work best
3. **Mix Content Types**: Variety keeps audience engaged
4. **Review Topics**: Ensure good mix of content

### Content Strategy

1. **Consistent Style**: Use same style for brand recognition
2. **Varied Topics**: Different angles on same niche
3. **Series Content**: Create related video series
4. **Evergreen + Trending**: Mix both types

### Optimization

1. **Monitor Tracker**: Check progress regularly
2. **Adjust if Needed**: Can modify schedules
3. **Track Performance**: See what works
4. **Iterate**: Improve next month's batch

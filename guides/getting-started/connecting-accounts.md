# Connecting Social Media Accounts

Connect all your social media accounts to enable automatic cross-posting.

## Why Connect Accounts?

- **One Video, Multiple Platforms**: Create once, post everywhere
- **Automatic Publishing**: No manual uploads needed
- **Consistent Branding**: Same content across all platforms
- **Time Savings**: Hours saved per week
- **Maximum Reach**: Reach all your audiences simultaneously

## Supported Platforms

- ✅ **YouTube**
- ✅ **TikTok**
- ✅ **Instagram**
- ✅ **Facebook**
- ✅ **Threads** (optional)
- ✅ **LinkedIn** (optional)

## How to Connect Accounts

### Step 1: Navigate to Accounts

1. Go to **"Accounts"** tab in left sidebar
2. Or click **"Connect Accounts"** from dashboard

### Step 2: Connect Each Platform

For each platform:

1. Click **"Connect [Platform]"**
2. You'll be redirected to platform login
3. Authorize Syllaby to access your account
4. Grant necessary permissions:
   - **Post videos**
   - **Manage content**
   - **View analytics** (optional)

### Step 3: Verify Connection

- Check that platform shows as "Connected"
- Green checkmark indicates success
- You can disconnect anytime

## Managing Connected Accounts

### View Connected Accounts
- Go to **"Accounts"** section
- See all connected platforms
- View connection status

### Disconnect Account
1. Go to **"Accounts"**
2. Click **"Disconnect"** next to platform
3. Confirm disconnection
4. Can reconnect anytime

### Reconnect Account
- If connection expires
- Click **"Reconnect"**
- Re-authorize access
- Usually happens automatically

## Troubleshooting

### Connection Issues

**Problem**: Can't connect account  
**Solution**: 
- Clear browser cache
- Try different browser
- Check platform permissions
- Contact support if persists

**Problem**: Connection expired  
**Solution**:
- Reconnect account
- Re-authorize permissions
- Usually automatic

**Problem**: Videos not posting  
**Solution**:
- Check account connection status
- Verify permissions granted
- Check platform-specific requirements
- Review error messages

# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is Syllaby.io?
Syllaby.io is an AI-powered video creation platform that transforms your ideas into complete, social media-ready videos. It handles everything from script generation to video production and multi-platform publishing.

### How much does it cost?
Syllaby offers flexible pricing starting at $25/month. Higher tiers provide more credits. There's also a free trial available with no credit card required.

### Do I need video editing experience?
No! Syllaby is designed for beginners. The interface is intuitive and guides you through every step. No video editing skills required.

### Can I use my own images?
Yes! You can upload your own images, photos, or graphics. Uploaded media is free to use and doesn't cost credits.

### Do I own the videos I create?
Yes! All videos you create are yours to download and use for any purpose, including commercial use. No watermarks.

## Account & Setup

### How do I create an account?
Visit [Syllaby.io](https://syllaby.io/?via=chris56) and click "Sign Up" or "Start Free Trial". No credit card required for the trial.

### Can I manage multiple channels?
Yes! One Gmail account can manage 20+ channels. Some users manage 700+ channels under one account.

### Do I need separate domains for each channel?
No, all channels can be under one email account. No need for separate .com domains.

### How do I connect social media accounts?
Go to the "Accounts" tab in your dashboard and click "Connect" for each platform. You'll be redirected to authorize access.

## Credits & Pricing

### How does the credit system work?
Credits are used to create videos and use features:
- Faceless Video: 13 credits per 1-minute video
- Idea Discovery: 15 credits per search
- Script Generation: 1 credit per 1-minute script
- Thumbnail Generation: 1 credit per 3 thumbnails

### Do credits rollover?
No, credits do not rollover each month. Use all credits before the billing cycle ends.

### What's free to use?
- Exporting videos after editing
- First 3 image regenerations per video
- Asset library (reusing previously created content)
- Uploaded media
- Stock videos

### How are credits charged?
Credits are charged per whole minute. For example, a 5:13 video = 6 minutes = 78 credits.

## Video Creation

### How long does video generation take?
Most videos take 1-2 minutes to generate. Longer videos may take slightly longer.

### Can I edit videos after generation?
Yes! Full editing capabilities are available. You can regenerate images, add motion, upload your own media, adjust subtitles, and change music.

### What video styles are available?
Four main styles:
1. AI Visuals (most popular)
2. Video Vault (consistent background)
3. B-Roll Videos (stock footage)
4. URL to Video (scrape from websites)

### How do I create longer videos?
Set the video length to 5-20 minutes in the video options. Longer videos use more credits but provide more watch time.

### Can I use character consistency?
Yes! Character consistency is available for certain art styles. Look for the purple icon on supported styles.

## Scripts & Content

### How do I create scripts?
Four options:
1. AI Script Assistant (recommended)
2. Custom script (write your own)
3. URL to Script (paste article URL)
4. Audio to Video (upload audio)

### Can I edit AI-generated scripts?
Yes! You can edit any script after generation. Full control over content.

### How do I find content ideas?
Use Idea Discovery feature:
- Enter a keyword or topic
- Get 10 trending ideas with data
- See search volume, trends, competition
- Create videos directly from ideas

### Can I repurpose existing content?
Yes! Use "URL to Script" to convert articles or blog posts into video scripts. Perfect for content repurposing.

## Monetization

### Does YouTube pay for AI-generated content?
Yes! YouTube confirmed they pay for AI-generated content. This was a rumor that has been debunked.

### How do I get monetized on YouTube?
Requirements:
- 1,000 subscribers
- 4,000 watch hours (long-form) OR 10 million Shorts views
- Follow Community Guidelines
- Live in eligible country

See [Getting Monetized Fast](../monetization/getting-monetized-fast.md) for strategies.

### How much can I earn?
Earnings vary by niche:
- Finance: $10-50 per 1,000 views
- Health: $3-10 per 1,000 views
- Technology: $5-20 per 1,000 views
- Business: $8-25 per 1,000 views

### What are the best revenue streams?
1. YouTube AdSense (base income)
2. Affiliate Marketing (higher potential)
3. Digital Products (courses, ebooks)
4. Brand Deals (sponsorships)
5. Services (coaching, consulting)

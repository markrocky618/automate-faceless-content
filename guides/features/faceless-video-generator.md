# Faceless Video Generator - Complete Guide

The Faceless Video Generator is Syllaby's most powerful feature. This comprehensive guide will teach you everything you need to know.

## Overview

The Faceless Video Generator is designed as a guided step-by-step workflow, perfect for both beginners and pros. It transforms your ideas into complete, professional videos in minutes.

## Four Video Style Options

### 1. AI Visuals (Most Popular)

**What It Is**:
- AI generates custom images based on your script
- Images are stitched into a full video
- Most versatile option

**Best For**:
- Most content types
- Character consistency
- Custom visuals
- Educational content

**Credits**: 13 credits per 1-minute video

### 2. Video Vault

**What It Is**:
- Choose from pre-selected B-roll background videos
- Select one video that stays consistent throughout
- Single visual theme

**Best For**:
- Consistent branding
- Background videos
- Simple content
- When you want one visual style

**Credits**: 13 credits per 1-minute video

### 3. B-Roll Videos

**What It Is**:
- Syllaby pulls relevant clips from stock library
- Automatically matches clips to your script
- Professional visual experience

**Best For**:
- Professional look
- Variety in visuals
- Stock footage content
- Documentary style

**Credits**: 13 credits per 1-minute video

### 4. URL to Video

**What It Is**:
- Scrapes images from any webpage or listing
- Perfect for affiliate marketers
- **No credits required** for image scraping

**Best For**:
- Product promotions
- Real estate listings
- Affiliate marketing
- Website promotions

**Credits**: Only video generation credits (13 per minute)

## Complete Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Schedule Setup

**What to Configure**:
- **Video Title**: SEO-optimized title
- **Description**: Auto-generate or custom
- **Tags**: Auto-generate or custom
- **Hashtags**: Platform-specific
- **CTAs**: Call-to-action links
- **Affiliate Links**: Product promotions

**Publishing Options**:
- **Publish Now**: Immediate posting
- **Schedule for Later**: Use content calendar
- **Bulk Schedule**: Create multiple videos

**Pro Tip**: Enable "Auto-generate AI description and tags" for better SEO.

### Step 2: Video Customization

**Language**:
- Choose from 25+ languages
- Affects voiceover and subtitles

**Voice Actor**:
- Multiple AI voices available
- Choose based on your niche
- Test different voices

**Subtitle Font & Style**:
- Customize appearance
- Position (top, middle, bottom)
- Animation styles

**Video Length**:
- **Short-form**: Under 1 minute (TikTok, Reels, Shorts)
- **Long-form**: 5-20 minutes (YouTube)

**Aspect Ratio**:
- **Portrait**: 9:16 (TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts)
- **Landscape**: 16:9 (YouTube, Facebook)
- **Square**: 1:1 (Instagram posts)

### Step 3: Script Creation (Four Options)

#### Option 1: Custom Script
- Write your own script
- Paste existing content
- Full control over content

#### Option 2: AI Script Assistant (Recommended)
- Enter a topic or question
- AI generates complete script
- Trained on top-performing scripts
- Based on proven formulas

**How to Use**:
1. Click "AI Script Assistant"
2. Enter your topic (e.g., "How to use AI for content creation")
3. Click "Generate Script"
4. Review and edit if needed

#### Option 3: URL to Script
- Paste any article or blog link
- Syllaby converts to video script
- Perfect for repurposing content

**How to Use**:
1. Click "URL to Script"
2. Paste article URL
3. Click "Generate Script from URL"
4. Review generated script

#### Option 4: Audio to Video
- Upload audio recording or voiceover
- Even upload songs
- Syllaby generates visuals to match

**How to Use**:
1. Click "Audio to Video"
2. Upload audio file
3. Generate visuals
4. Video created automatically

### Step 4: Advanced Options

**Subtitle Positioning**:
- Top, middle, or bottom
- Custom positioning available

**Background Music**:
- Multiple music options
- Match to your content style
- Adjustable volume

**Video Filters**:
- Apply visual filters
- Enhance appearance
- Style customization

**Visual Effects**:
- Animation effects
- Transitions
- Special effects

### Step 5: Generate Video

**Before Generating**:
- Review summary (length, credits)
- Check all settings
- Verify script

**Generate Process**:
1. Click "Generate Video" (bottom right)
2. System renders in background
3. Wait 1-2 minutes
4. Video appears in "My Videos" section

**After Generation**:
- Syllaby shows **5 new topic ideas**
- Related to your video topic
- Click any idea to create next video
- Seamless content pipeline

## Pro Tips

### Content Creation
1. **Start with AI Script Assistant**: Saves time, proven formulas
2. **Test Different Styles**: See what resonates with audience
3. **Use Character Consistency**: Build brand recognition

2. **Descriptions**: Auto-generate for better SEO
3. **Tags**: Relevant tags for discoverability
4. **Thumbnails**: Create eye-catching thumbnails
